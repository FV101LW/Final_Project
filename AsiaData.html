Hall of Endangered Species

Asia

Taiwanese White Dolphin

Binomial Name: Sousa chinensis taiwanensis

Native Area: Taiwan

Total Population: ~60

Status: Vulnerable

Threats: Air and water pollution, habitat loss, by-catch from gillnets

Japanese Eel

Binomial Name: Anguilla japonica

Native Area: East & Southeast Asia

Total Population: Unknown

Status: Endangered

Threats: Overfishing, Habitat loss

Caspian Lamprey

Binomial Name: Caspiomyzon wagneri

Native Area: Russia

Total Population: Unknown

Status: Near Threatened

Threats: Drying of Stream via Drought, Infrastructure Projects(Dams)

Irrawaddy Dolphin

Binomial Name: Orcaella brevirostris

Native Area: South & Southeast Asia

Total Population: ~7000

Status: Endangered

Threats: Drowning in nets, Dams, Electro-fishing, Blast Fishing, Noise Pollution, Gold mining

Yangtze Finless Porpoise

Binomial Name: Neophocaena asiaeorientalis

Native Area: Yangtze River, China

Total Population: ~7000

Status: Critically Endangered

Threats: Dams, Illegal Fishing, Pollution, Sand mining

Yellow Tube Sponge

Binomial Name: Aplysina fistularis

Native Area: Bikini Atoll

Total Population: Unknown

Status: Least Concern

Threats: Habitat changes, water pollution
