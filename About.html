About Us

Our project's mission is to provide an opportunity for volunteers from all sorts of backgrounds to coordinate and conduct activities for the preservation of ocean life and ecosystems.

Location

Mainly based in Asia.

Our Motivation

Problems of Our Seas

The Earth's bodies of water have been under threat for several decades, mostly by human activities. Many individuals and groups have launched efforts to address the issue of our oceans' well-being. We intend to join this movement and establish a group for those who wish to contribute to the protection of aquatic life.

SDG 14: Life Below Water

SDG 14 aims to conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources for sustainable development. It addresses issues such as marine pollution, overfishing, and the preservation of marine biodiversity. Through collective efforts, we hope to contribute to achieving this global goal.
