About Us

Our project's mission is to provide an opportunity for volunteers from all sorts of backgrounds to be able to coordinate and conduct activities for the preservation of ocean life and ecosystem.

Location:

Mainly based in Asia.

Our Motivation

Problems of our Seas:

The Earth's bodies of water have been under threat for several decades, mostly by human activities. Many individuals and groups have launched efforts to address the issue of our oceans' well-being. We intend to join this part and establish a group, a movement for those who wish to step up and contribute in the protection of aquatic life.

SDG 14: Life Below Water
